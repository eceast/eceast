The exhibition centre structure

“The noncommercial organization (establishment) (NCO(E))
"Saint-Petersburg exhibition centre
named after L.I. Brezhnev"
at "Exhibition of achievements of science and technology
named after A.A. Sobchak"”
includes administrative-scientific divisions (I),
scientific-organizational divisions (II),
administrative-economy divisions (III),
scientific-research and educational activity
exhibitions (financing) divisions (IV),
fundamental scientific researches
exhibitions (financing) divisions (IV.A),
applied scientific researches
exhibitions (financing) divisions (IV.B),
divisions of scientific centre (additional) (V),
scientific-historical divisions (additional) (VI),
scientific-publical divisions (additional) (VII),
divisions of “SIO
"Academy of cognitive natural sciences"” (additional) (VIII)
and other divisions.
