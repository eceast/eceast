socialist republic (RSFSR)

3.2.Data about Sobchak Anatoly Aleksandrovich

Sobchak Anatoly Aleksandrovich
(the 10th of August 1937 y. –
the 19th of February 2000 y.) –
soviet and russian politician,
first and still only mayor of Saint-Petersburg city.
In 1956 y. he was arrived on law department
of The Leningrad state university (LSU).
Since 1959 y., after the termination of university,
on distribution he was worked as the lawyer
in The Stavropol regional bar,
then manager of legal consultation
in Stavropol krai.
In 1962 y. he was returned to Leningrad city.
He was ended postgraduate study of LSU.
In 1964 y. he was defended the candidate dissertation
on a subject “Civil-law responsibility
for infliction of harm by action
of source of the increased danger”.
From 1965 y. to 1968 y. he was taught
at The Leningrad special school of militia
of The Ministry of internal affairs (MIA)
of USSR.
In 1968 y. – 1973 y. – associate professor
of The Leningrad institute of technology
of pulp and paper industry.
In 1973 y. he was didn't defend
the doctoral dissertation
on theme “Legal problems of the economic
calculation in the industry of USSR”.
From 1973 y. to 1981 y. – the associate professor
of The Law department of LSU,
since 1982 y. – the doctor of jurisprudence
and professor of the same faculty,
where since 1985 y. he was held a position
of the head of the department of economic right.
The member of CPSU since 1988 y.,
he was left from CPSU in 1990 y.
In 1989 y. he was elected to The People's deputy
of USSR.
At the first congress he was a part
of The Supreme council of USSR.
He was The chairman of subcommittee
of SC of USSR
by the economic legislation of The Committee
on the legislation, legality and law (order).
In June 1989 y. he was a part
Interregional deputy group.
He was entered into the commission on investigation
of Tbilisi events in April 1989 y.
He was claimed that at meeting dispersal by forces
of The Soviet army engineer shovels were used.
He was became (later) the honourable citizen
of Tbilisi city and Georgia country.
In April 1990 y. he was elected the deputy
of Leningrad city soviet.
On 23rd of May 1990 y. he was elected the chairman
of The Leningrad city soviet.
On the 12th of June 1991 y. Sobchak A.A. was elected
to the mayor of Leningrad city on the elections passing
along with The Russian presidential elections.
At the same time on a referendum the decision was made
about return to Leningrad name of The Saint-Petersburg.
In July 1991 y. he was acted as one of founders
of movements of democratic reforms.
Actively he was opposed actions
of The State committee on state
of emergency (SCSE)
in the August 1991 y. and actually he was headed
resistance to putshists in Leningrad city.
Sobchak A.A. actively participated in process
of creations of the new Constitution of Russia.
In October 1993 he was headed party list of candidates
in The State duma
from The Russian movement of democratic reforms.
On elections on the 12th of December 1993 y. the block
didn't gain necessary number of votes
for passing to The State duma.
Since 1994 y. Anatoly Sobchak was
The Prime minister of Saint-Petersburg city.
In February 1996 y. he was entered
in The Petersburg office of The movement
“Our home is Russia”.
On the 16th of June 1996 y. he was lost elections
of The Governor of Saint-Petersburg city
to the deputy Vladimir Yakovlev.
He was lived in Paris city till the 12th of July 1999 y.
He was gave lectures in to Sorbonne city
and other French universities.
On the 21st of December 1999 y. he was lost elections
in deputies of The State duma to the candidate of “Apple”
to Pyotr Shelishchu also he was declared,
that made the decision to participate
in elections of The Governor of Saint-Petersburg city.
On the 14th of February 2000 y. he was appointed
authorized representative of the candidate
in The Presidents of The Russian Federation Putin V.V.,
also he was headed The Political advisory board
of democratic parties and movements
of Saint- Petersburg city.
Sobchak A.A. has the state awards
of The Russian Federation:
1 anniversary medal “300 years of The Russian fleet”,
1 Olympic order,
1 award of The Saint blessed prince
Daniel of Moscow of the Ist degree,
1 breastplate “Honourable citizen
of Saint-Petersburg city”.
